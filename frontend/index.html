Ken Fried

Founder & Managing Partner

Ken Fried is the founder and Managing Partner of OpenDoor Venture Capital, bringing over two decades of experience in technology investments and entrepreneurship. With a particular focus on transformative technologies in blockchain and artificial intelligence, Ken has established himself as a leading voice in early-stage technology investments.

Prior to founding OpenDoor VC, Ken held senior positions at leading technology companies and investment firms, where he developed a deep understanding of emerging technologies and market dynamics. His investment philosophy centers on identifying and supporting visionary founders who are building the next generation of groundbreaking technologies.
